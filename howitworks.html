Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a computer science degree?

No! Most of our students come from non-technical backgrounds. We teach you everything from the ground up.

Can I work while studying?

Yes! Our part-time programs are designed for working professionals with evening and weekend classes.

What if I fall behind?

We offer catch-up sessions, 1-on-1 mentoring, and extended access to materials. We don't leave anyone behind.

Do you help with job placement?

Absolutely! Career services include resume reviews, interview prep, and direct connections to our 800+ employer partners.

What's the time commitment?

Full-time: 40+ hours/week. Part-time: 15-20 hours/week. Choose what works for your schedule.

Is there financing available?

Yes! We offer payment plans, income share agreements, and need-based scholarships for qualified students.
